# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Product Owners

### Role Summary
Product Owners define and prioritize the product backlog to maximize business value. They ensure the team's work aligns with customer needs and stakeholder expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Manage and prioritize the product backlog
- Gather and document user requirements
- Define and clarify acceptance criteria
- Make prioritization decisions balancing business value and technical constraints
- Serve as the voice of the customer and stakeholders

### Goals
- Maximize business value delivered in each iteration
- Ensure alignment between development work and business objectives
- Maintain a clear, prioritized backlog ready for development

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and backlog refinement sessions
- Regular stakeholder meetings to gather feedback
- Collaboration with UX/UI Designer on user needs
- Alignment meetings with Project Manager on delivery priorities

### Key Interactions
- Works closely with **Project Manager** to align priorities with delivery capacity
- Collaborates with **UX/UI Designer** to integrate user research into requirements
- Partners with **Business Analyst** to refine and document requirements
- Coordinates with **Developers** to clarify acceptance criteria and provide feedback

---

## Quality Assurance Leads

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Leads establish quality standards and coordinate validation efforts to ensure deliverables meet acceptance criteria and performance expectations.

### Responsibilities
- Define quality standards and acceptance criteria
- Establish comprehensive test strategies and plans
- Coordinate testing efforts across teams
- Lead defect triage and resolution prioritization
- Ensure test coverage meets quality benchmarks

### Goals
- Maintain high quality standards across all deliverables
- Reduce defects reaching production
- Enable rapid feedback cycles through effective testing

### Typical Communication
- Daily coordination with Developers on test progress
- Quality reviews during sprint demos
- Test plan documentation and status reports
- Defect triage meetings with Product Owner and Developers

### Key Interactions
- Collaborates with **Developers** to implement test automation and resolve defects
- Works with **Product Owner** to validate acceptance criteria are met
- Partners with **DevOps Specialist** to integrate testing into CI/CD pipelines
- Coordinates with **Change Manager** to ensure quality expectations for releases

---

## Business Analysts

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge requirements between business stakeholders and technical teams, ensuring clarity in scope, requirements, and acceptance criteria.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements
- Translate business needs into actionable technical requirements
- Clarify and refine acceptance criteria
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops
- Validate that implemented solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure requirements are clearly understood by all parties
- Minimize rework due to unclear or incomplete requirements
- Facilitate effective communication between business and technical teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders
- Documentation of user stories and acceptance criteria
- Clarification sessions with Developers and Product Owner
- Requirements validation and walkthroughs

### Key Interactions
- Partners with **Product Owner** to refine backlog items and acceptance criteria
- Works with **Developers** to clarify technical implementation of requirements
- Collaborates with **UX/UI Designer** to ensure requirements support good user experience
- Coordinates with **Project Manager** on scope and timeline implications

---

## Change Managers

### Role Summary
Change Managers plan, communicate, and support organizational change required by projects, ensuring smooth adoption and minimizing disruption.

### Responsibilities
- Develop change communication and adoption plans
- Analyze change impact on users and processes
- Create training materials and support documentation
- Monitor adoption metrics and user feedback
- Support stakeholders through transition periods

### Goals
- Maximize user adoption and minimize resistance
- Reduce disruption during transitions
- Enable successful organizational change outcomes

### Typical Communication
- Change impact assessments and readiness reports
- Training sessions and adoption workshops
- Stakeholder communications on upcoming changes
- Post-deployment adoption metrics and feedback

### Key Interactions
- Partners with **Project Manager** throughout the change lifecycle
- Works with **Quality Assurance Lead** to ensure quality supports adoption
- Collaborates with **Product Owner** and **UX/UI Designer** on user experience
- Coordinates with stakeholders to manage expectations and gather feedback

---

## DevOps Specialists

### Role Summary
DevOps Specialists implement CI/CD pipelines, manage infrastructure, and ensure reliable, automated software delivery and operations.

### Responsibilities
- Implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage cloud infrastructure and deployment automation
- Monitor system reliability, performance, and availability
- Ensure security best practices in deployment processes
- Support incident response and troubleshooting

### Goals
- Enable rapid, reliable software delivery
- Minimize deployment risks and downtime
- Maintain high system availability and performance

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure and deployment planning sessions
- Incident response and post-mortem reviews
- CI/CD pipeline status and improvement proposals
- Technical coordination with Developers and QA

### Key Interactions
- Collaborates closely with **Developers** to streamline build and deployment processes
- Works with **Quality Assurance Lead** to integrate automated testing
- Coordinates with **Project Manager** on release timelines and deployment windows
- Partners with Operations teams to ensure production support and monitoring

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers ensure user experience and interface design are integrated throughout the project, advocating for user needs and usability best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Design user flows, wireframes, and visual assets
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create design systems and maintain consistency
- Advocate for user needs throughout development
- Validate implemented designs meet usability standards

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across features
- Incorporate user feedback into design iterations

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and recommendations
- Prototype walkthroughs with stakeholders
- Design specification documentation

### Key Interactions
- Partners with **Product Owner** to understand user needs and business goals
- Collaborates with **Developers** to ensure design feasibility and implementation fidelity
- Works with **Business Analyst** to incorporate usability into requirements
- Coordinates with **Quality Assurance Lead** on usability testing
